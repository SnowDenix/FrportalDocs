## Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive overview of store operations standards, procedures, and best practices for all Aydınlı Group franchise stores. Effective store operations ensure consistent customer experiences, operational efficiency, and business profitability across all locations.

### Operations Mission

Deliver exceptional customer experiences through:
- **Consistency:** Standardized operations across all stores
- **Efficiency:** Optimized processes and resource utilization
- **Compliance:** Adherence to brand and regulatory standards
- **Excellence:** Continuous improvement in service delivery

|------|----------|-----------|

### Prohibited Actions

- ❌ Personal mobile phone use on sales floor
- ❌ Eating or drinking on sales floor
- ❌ Unauthorized discounts
- ❌ Cash register access without authorization
- ❌ Sharing passwords or PINs
- ❌ Leaving store unattended
- ❌ Employee purchases during shift
- ❌ Discussing confidential information

## Checklists

### Daily Opening Checklist

```
SECURITY & SYSTEMS
□ Deactivate alarm using authorized code
□ Check for signs of break-in or damage
□ Turn on all lights (sales floor, stockroom)
□ Power on POS systems
□ Log into all required systems

CASH & BANKING
□ Retrieve cash float from safe
□ Count and verify float amount
□ Place float in register(s)
□ Record opening cash on register

STORE PRESENTATION
□ Full store walkthrough
□ Check all displays for integrity
□ Verify mannequin styling
□ Check fitting rooms (clear, clean)
□ Ensure music is playing at correct level

TEAM & COMMUNICATION
□ Review previous day's performance
□ Check for HQ communications
□ Assign zones and tasks to team
□ Brief team on daily targets and focus
```

### Daily Closing Checklist

```
CUSTOMER & SALES FLOOR
□ Assist final customers
□ Close fitting rooms 15 min before close
□ Final fitting room check and clear
□ Lock entrance at closing time

CASH & REPORTING
□ Complete all pending transactions
□ Run Z-report
□ Count cash and reconcile
□ Prepare bank deposit
□ Secure cash in safe

STORE SECURITY
□ Full store walkthrough
□ Check all windows and doors
□ Turn off non-essential lights
□ Power down POS (per local protocol)
□ Set alarm
□ Double-check all locks

NEXT DAY PREPARATION
□ Note any issues for next shift
□ Check staffing schedule
□ Prepare special tasks list
```

## Examples / Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Customer Complaint Handling

**Situation:** Customer unhappy with product quality

**Process:**
```
Step 1: ACKNOWLEDGE
├── Listen actively without interruption
├── Show empathy: "I understand your frustration"
├── Thank customer for bringing to attention
└── Move to private area if needed

Step 2: INVESTIGATE
├── Examine the product
├── Review purchase history
├── Understand customer's desired outcome
└── Check policy applicability

Step 3: RESOLVE
├── Offer solution within authority
├── If exceeds authority, involve manager
├── Execute agreed solution
├── Document the incident
└── Follow up if promised

Step 4: RECOVER
├── Apologize for inconvenience
├── Offer goodwill gesture if appropriate
├── Thank customer for patience
└── Invite to return
```

### Scenario 2: Staff Shortage

**Situation:** Team member calls in sick on Saturday

**Response Protocol:**
1. Contact replacement list (within 1 hour)
2. Adjust zone assignments if no replacement
3. Inform Regional Manager if critical
4. Prioritize customer-facing activities
5. Postpone non-essential tasks
6. Document for scheduling review

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Low Conversion Rate

**Problem:** High traffic but low sales

**Solutions:**
- Improve greeting and engagement
- Better product knowledge training
- Review fitting room conversion
- Analyze customer journey obstacles
- Enhance VM to guide customers

---

### Issue 2: Cash Discrepancies

**Problem:** Register over/short at closing

**Solutions:**
- Re-count with witness
- Review transaction history
- Check for voided transactions
- Verify all tender types
- Document and report per policy

---

### Issue 3: Stock Availability Issues

**Problem:** Customer wants item not in stock

**Solutions:**
- Check other stores immediately
- Offer to order/transfer
- Suggest alternatives
- Take customer details for callback
- Log for replenishment review
