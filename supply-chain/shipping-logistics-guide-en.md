## Purpose

This document provides comprehensive guidance on shipping and logistics operations for franchise stores. Understanding shipping processes, carrier management, and logistics coordination ensures efficient product movement and timely store replenishment.

### Shipment Status Meanings

```
SHIPMENT STATUS GUIDE

ORDER PLACED
└── Order received and processing

PICKED & PACKED
└── Items ready for shipment

SHIPPED / IN TRANSIT
└── Handed to carrier, en route
    ├── DEPARTED - Left origin facility
    ├── IN TRANSIT - Moving through network
    ├── AT HUB - At carrier sorting facility
    └── OUT FOR DELIVERY - On delivery vehicle

DELIVERED
└── Received at destination

EXCEPTION / DELAY
└── Issue requiring attention
    ├── ADDRESS ISSUE - Delivery address problem
    ├── WEATHER DELAY - Weather-related delay
    ├── CUSTOMS HOLD - Held for customs clearance
    └── RECIPIENT UNAVAILABLE - No one to receive
```

## Examples / Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Tracking Standard Domestic Shipment

**Situation:** Weekly replenishment from Istanbul DC

**Process:**
```
Day 1 (Monday):
1. Receive ASN email with tracking info
2. Note tracking number: TRK123456789
3. Expected delivery: Thursday

Day 2-3:
4. Check tracking daily
5. Status: "In Transit - Ankara Hub"
6. On schedule

Day 4 (Thursday):
7. Status: "Out for Delivery"
8. Prepare receiving area
9. Receive shipment at 14:30
10. Verify 15 boxes as expected
11. Sign POD
12. Confirm in system
```

---

### Scenario 2: Handling Delivery Delay

**Situation:** Shipment delayed due to weather

**Process:**
```
1. Tracking shows "Weather Delay"
2. Original ETA: Wednesday
3. New ETA: Friday
4. Actions taken:
   - Notify store manager
   - Adjust staffing schedule
   - Inform customers if needed
   - Document delay
5. Monitor for updates
6. Receive shipment Friday
7. Report delay for records
```

---

### Scenario 3: International Customs Clearance

**Situation:** Shipment held at customs

**Process:**
```
1. Tracking shows "Customs Hold"
2. Contact freight forwarder
3. Identify issue: Missing document
4. HQ provides required certificate
5. Submit to customs broker
6. Customs releases shipment
7. Continue to destination
8. Document issue for future reference
```

---

### Scenario 4: Damaged Shipment Claim

**Situation:** Delivery arrives with visible damage

**Process:**
```
1. Note damage on delivery receipt
2. Photograph damaged packaging
3. Open and inspect contents
4. Document all damaged items
5. Complete damage report form
6. Submit to:
   - Carrier for claim
   - HQ for replacement
   - Insurance if applicable
7. Hold damaged goods
8. Track claim resolution
9. Receive credit/replacement
```

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Repeated Delivery Delays

**Problem:** Specific route consistently late

**Solutions:**
- Review carrier performance data
- Request carrier investigation
- Consider alternative carriers
- Adjust expected delivery times
- Plan inventory buffer

---

### Issue 2: Frequent Damage Claims

**Problem:** Products arriving damaged

**Solutions:**
- Review packaging standards
- Request enhanced packaging
- Document and report patterns
- Change carrier if needed
- Implement damage prevention measures

---

### Issue 3: Tracking Information Not Updating

**Problem:** Cannot get shipment status

**Solutions:**
- Contact carrier directly
- Verify tracking number
- Check for system issues
- Request manual status update
- Escalate if unresolved

---

### Issue 4: Customs Delays

**Problem:** International shipments stuck in customs

**Solutions:**
- Ensure documentation complete
- Work with experienced broker
- Pre-clear paperwork when possible
- Understand destination requirements
- Maintain compliance records

## Best Practices

### Efficient Logistics Management

1. **Proactive Tracking**
   - Check status daily
   - Set up alerts
   - Act on exceptions immediately

2. **Communication**
   - Notify team of deliveries
   - Report issues promptly
   - Confirm receipt same day

3. **Documentation**
   - Keep all shipping documents
   - Photo any damage immediately
   - File claims within deadlines

4. **Continuous Improvement**
   - Track patterns in delays
   - Provide carrier feedback
   - Suggest process improvements
