## Document Overview

This comprehensive operations documentation covers all aspects of store operations and supply chain management for Aydınlı Group franchise stores. The document is organized into 13 major sections following a logical hierarchy from overview to specific procedures:

**Section 1: Store Operations Overview** - Presents the complete operational framework, organizational structure, key responsibilities, and daily workflow processes that govern store management and staff coordination.

**Section 2: Supply Chain Overview** - Provides comprehensive view of the supply chain ecosystem including vendor relationships, distribution network, inventory flow, and coordination between stores, warehouses, and headquarters.

**Section 3: Opening and Closing Procedures** - Provides step-by-step checklists for daily store opening and closing operations including system checks, cash drawer setup, security protocols, and end-of-day reconciliation processes.

**Section 4: Cash Management Guide** - Details all cash handling procedures including register operations, safe management, bank deposits, change ordering, variance handling, and fraud prevention protocols. Sets standards for float amounts and daily reconciliation procedures.

**Section 5: Customer Service Standards** - Establishes service excellence standards including greeting protocols, complaint handling, service recovery procedures, and customer interaction guidelines. Defines service level expectations and escalation procedures.

**Section 6: Inventory Control Guide** - Covers inventory management processes including stock counting methods, shrinkage control, cycle counting procedures, stock accuracy targets, and inventory reporting requirements for maintaining optimal stock levels.

**Section 7: Loss Prevention Guide** - Outlines security measures, theft prevention strategies, employee awareness protocols, surveillance systems usage, and incident reporting procedures to minimize shrinkage and protect store assets.

**Section 8: Return and Exchange Procedures** - Details policies and processes for handling customer returns, exchanges, and refunds including approval requirements, documentation needs, and quality control checks for returned merchandise.

**Section 9: Order Management Guide** - Covers the complete order lifecycle from creation to fulfillment including order placement procedures, tracking systems, communication protocols, and order issue resolution.

**Section 10: Delivery and Receiving Procedures** - Explains incoming shipment handling including delivery acceptance, quality inspection, documentation requirements, discrepancy resolution, and proper storage of received goods.

**Section 11: Stock Transfer Procedures** - Describes inter-store and warehouse transfer processes including transfer request procedures, approval workflows, documentation requirements, and inventory reconciliation for transferred goods.

**Section 12: Shipping and Logistics Guide** - Details outbound shipping processes including packaging standards, carrier selection, shipping documentation, tracking procedures, and logistics coordination with distribution centers.

**Section 13: Warehouse Coordination Guide** - Explains communication and coordination protocols with central warehouses including stock request procedures, delivery scheduling, emergency stock processes, and warehouse system usage.

## Checklists

### Daily Opening Checklist

```
SECURITY & SYSTEMS
□ Deactivate alarm using authorized code
□ Check for signs of break-in or damage
□ Turn on all lights (sales floor, stockroom)
□ Power on POS systems
□ Log into all required systems

CASH & BANKING
□ Retrieve cash float from safe
□ Count and verify float amount
□ Place float in register(s)
□ Record opening cash on register

STORE PRESENTATION
□ Full store walkthrough
□ Check all displays for integrity
□ Verify mannequin styling
□ Check fitting rooms (clear, clean)
□ Ensure music is playing at correct level

TEAM & COMMUNICATION
□ Review previous day's performance
□ Check for HQ communications
□ Assign zones and tasks to team
□ Brief team on daily targets and focus
```

### Daily Closing Checklist

```
CUSTOMER & SALES FLOOR
□ Assist final customers
□ Close fitting rooms 15 min before close
□ Final fitting room check and clear
□ Lock entrance at closing time

CASH & REPORTING
□ Complete all pending transactions
□ Run Z-report
□ Count cash and reconcile
□ Prepare bank deposit
□ Secure cash in safe

STORE SECURITY
□ Full store walkthrough
□ Check all windows and doors
□ Turn off non-essential lights
□ Power down POS (per local protocol)

□ Double-check all locks

NEXT DAY PREPARATION
□ Note any issues for next shift
```

## Examples / Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Alarm Triggers During Opening

**Situation:** Alarm sounds when entering store

**Response Protocol:**
1. Do NOT proceed into store
2. Exit immediately to safe distance
3. Call alarm monitoring company
4. Call police if advised
5. Call Store Manager / Regional Manager
6. Do not re-enter until cleared
7. Document incident fully

### Scenario 2: Customer Refuses to Leave at Closing

**Situation:** Customer still shopping 10 minutes past close

**Response Protocol:**
1. Politely approach customer
2. "I'm sorry, we've now closed. May I help you complete your purchase?"
3. Offer to hold items if needed
4. Escort to checkout
5. If refuses, involve manager
6. Never physically force customer
7. Document if significant delay

### Scenario 3: Cash Variance at Closing

**Situation:** $15 short in cash drawer

**Response Protocol:**
1. Re-count entire drawer
2. Check for miscounted bills
3. Review void/return transactions
4. Check for bills stuck together
5. Document variance on form
6. Report to Manager immediately
7. Complete incident report
8. Do not adjust without authorization


## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Alarm Won't Deactivate

**Solution:**
1. Try code again carefully
2. Check for correct code
3. Call alarm company immediately
4. Have ID ready for verification
5. Do not disable manually

### Issue 2: POS System Won't Start

**Solution:**
1. Check power connections
2. Try restart
3. Call IT support
4. Use backup procedures
5. Manual transaction recording if needed

### Issue 3: Cash Float Incorrect

**Solution:**
1. Re-count with witness
2. Check previous day's closing
3. Contact manager
4. Document discrepancy
5. Do not adjust without approval

## Best Practices

### Effective Communication

1. **Be Clear and Specific**
   - Provide order/reference numbers
   - Include all relevant details
   - Attach supporting documentation

2. **Use Right Channel**
   - Standard: B2B Portal
   - Urgent: Phone
   - Complex: Email with follow-up

3. **Document Everything**
   - Save ticket numbers
   - Note conversation details
   - File all correspondence

4. **Follow Up Consistently**
   - Check status regularly
   - Escalate when needed
   - Confirm resolution
