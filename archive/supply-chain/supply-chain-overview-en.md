## Purpose

This document provides a comprehensive overview of supply chain and logistics operations for Aydınlı Group franchise partners. Efficient supply chain management ensures product availability, inventory optimization, and customer satisfaction across all retail locations.

### Supply Chain Mission

"Deliver the right product, to the right place, at the right time, in the right quantity, at the right cost."

### Key Objectives

| Objective | Impact |
|-----------|--------|
| **Product Availability** | Minimize stock-outs |
| **Inventory Optimization** | Reduce overstock |
| **Speed to Market** | Fast replenishment |
| **Cost Efficiency** | Minimize logistics costs |
| **Accuracy** | Error-free deliveries |

## Examples / Scenarios

### Scenario 1: Urgent Stock Request

**Situation:** Key items running low before weekend

**Process:**
```
1. Check regional inventory (other stores)
2. If available: Request inter-store transfer
3. If not available: Contact Regional Manager
4. Request expedited shipment from DC
5. Confirm availability and ETA
6. Arrange express delivery if critical
7. Update inventory planning
```

### Scenario 2: Delivery with Shortages

**Situation:** Received 80 units, expected 100

**Process:**
```
1. Document shortage on delivery receipt
2. Take photos of all documentation
3. Complete discrepancy form
4. Submit to HQ within 24 hours
5. Do NOT adjust inventory for missing items
6. Wait for resolution from HQ
7. Follow up if no response in 48 hours
```

### Scenario 3: Damaged Goods in Shipment

**Situation:** 5 items damaged during shipping

**Process:**
```
1. Photograph damaged items
2. Document on receiving report
3. Separate from good inventory
4. Complete damage claim form
5. Submit to HQ and carrier
6. Hold damaged goods for inspection
7. Await replacement or credit
```

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Frequent Stock-outs

**Problem:** Key items regularly out of stock

**Solutions:**
- Review reorder points
- Increase safety stock levels
- Improve demand forecasting
- Request priority allocation
- Consider store transfers

---

### Issue 2: Delivery Delays

**Problem:** Shipments arriving late

**Solutions:**
- Track shipments proactively
- Communicate with carrier
- Plan buffer time
- Consider alternate carriers
- Escalate recurring issues

---

### Issue 3: Order Errors

**Problem:** Receiving wrong items/quantities

**Solutions:**
- Verify orders before submission
- Double-check receiving
- Report discrepancies immediately
- Request investigation
- Review order process
