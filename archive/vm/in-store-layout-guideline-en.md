## Purpose

This document establishes comprehensive guidelines for in-store layout planning, zone allocation, and fixture placement across all Aydınlı Group franchise stores. Strategic store layout maximizes customer engagement, optimizes traffic flow, and drives sales performance.

### Layout Objectives

| Objective | Business Impact |
|-----------|-----------------|
| **Optimize Traffic Flow** | Increased dwell time, exposure to more products |
| **Maximize Selling Space** | Higher sales per square meter |
| **Guide Customer Journey** | Strategic product discovery |
| **Enable Cross-Merchandising** | Higher units per transaction |
| **Support Brand Experience** | Premium brand perception |

## Definitions

| Term (EN) | Term (TR) | Definition |
|-----------|-----------|------------|
| A-Zone | A-Bölgesi | Highest traffic area near entrance |
| B-Zone | B-Bölgesi | Secondary traffic area in mid-store |
| C-Zone | C-Bölgesi | Lower traffic area toward back |
| Decompression Zone | Dekompresyon Alanı | First 1-2m where customers adjust |
| Power Wall | Güç Duvarı | High-impact wall visible from entrance |
| Speed Bump | Hız Kesici | Fixture that slows customer movement |
| Destination Zone | Hedef Bölge | Area with must-have products |
| Impulse Zone | Anlık Satın Alma Bölgesi | High-conversion areas near checkout |
| Gondola | Gondol | Free-standing double-sided fixture |
| Perimeter | Çevre | Wall-mounted fixtures around store |
| Adjacency | Komşuluk | Strategic placement of related categories |

## Standards & Rules

### Fixture Placement Rules

#### Wall Fixtures (Perimeter)
```
WALL FIXTURE HEIGHT ZONES

    ┌─────────────────────────────────┐
    │   UPPER ZONE (170+ cm)          │ ← Stock, Visual Display
    │   Display only, not for selling │
    ├─────────────────────────────────┤
    │   PREMIUM ZONE (145-170 cm)     │ ← Eye Level
    │   New season, highest margin    │    Best sellers
    ├─────────────────────────────────┤
    │   TOUCH ZONE (90-145 cm)        │ ← Easy reach
    │   Core products, easy access    │    High turnover
    ├─────────────────────────────────┤
    │   LOWER ZONE (0-90 cm)          │ ← Bulk items
    │   Bulky items, less visibility  │    Lower priority
    └─────────────────────────────────┘
```

#### Floor Fixtures
| Fixture | Use | Placement |
|---------|-----|-----------|
| **Gondola** | High capacity | Center aisles, B-Zone |
| **4-Way** | Featured items | A-Zone, intersections |
| **Round Rail** | Volume display | B/C-Zone |
| **Nesting Tables** | Folded items | A-Zone focal points |
| **Display Cube** | Hero products | Entrance, windows |

### Product Adjacency Matrix

| Category | Should Be Near | Should Avoid |
|----------|----------------|--------------|
| **Polo Shirts** | Casual trousers, belts | Formal suits |
| **Dress Shirts** | Ties, suits, dress trousers | Sportswear |
| **Casual Pants** | Polo, t-shirts, belts | Formal wear |
| **Suits** | Dress shirts, ties, shoes | Casual wear |
| **Accessories** | Related apparel, checkout | Isolated areas |
| **Outerwear** | Knitwear, scarves | Summer items |

### Space Allocation by Season

| Season | A-Zone Focus | B-Zone Focus | C-Zone |
|--------|--------------|--------------|--------|
| **Spring Launch** | New SS collection | Transitional | Core basics |
| **Summer** | Summer essentials | Light layers | Year-round |
| **Fall Launch** | New FW collection | Outerwear | Core basics |
| **Winter** | Cold weather | Gifts, layers | Essentials |
| **Sale Periods** | Marked-down hero | Promotional | Clearance |

## Checklists

### Weekly Layout Audit Checklist

```
ZONE COMPLIANCE
□ A-Zone contains new season/hero products
□ B-Zone has seasonal merchandise
□ C-Zone houses basics/essentials
□ Product adjacencies follow guidelines
□ No brand mixing (unless approved)

TRAFFIC FLOW
□ Main aisles minimum 120cm
□ Secondary aisles minimum 90cm
□ No blocked pathways
□ Clear sight lines to back of store
□ Decompression zone is product-free

FIXTURE POSITIONING
□ 4-Ways in designated locations
□ Gondolas properly aligned
□ Wall fixtures at correct heights
□ Tables positioned per planogram
□ No overcrowding

CASH WRAP AREA
□ Impulse products displayed
□ Queue space adequate
□ Accessories visible
□ Clear checkout signage

FITTING ROOM ZONE
□ Clear access path
□ Waiting area available
□ Related accessories nearby
□ Upsell products positioned
```

### Seasonal Reset Checklist

```
PRE-RESET PREPARATION
□ Review new planogram from HQ
□ Inventory new season products
□ Plan fixture movements
□ Schedule team and timing
□ Prepare signage and POP

RESET EXECUTION
□ Remove previous season displays
□ Clean all fixtures
□ Position fixtures per plan
□ Stock new products by zone
□ Apply size sequencing
□ Install signage
□ Adjust lighting

POST-RESET VERIFICATION
□ Compare to planogram
□ Walk customer journey
□ Check all sight lines
□ Verify pricing
□ Photo documentation
□ Report to HQ
```

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Dead Zones

**Problem:** Areas with low traffic and poor sales

**Solution:**
- Add focal point or hero product
- Improve sight lines with mirrors
- Create speed bump with featured display
- Enhance lighting

---

### Issue 2: Overcrowded A-Zone

**Problem:** Too many fixtures/products blocking flow

**Solution:**
- Remove 20-30% of fixtures
- Prioritize hero products only
- Create breathing space
- Follow capacity guidelines

---

### Issue 3: Poor Customer Flow

**Problem:** Customers not exploring entire store

**Solution:**
- Create clear pathway with flooring/lighting
- Position destination products (basics) in back
- Add visual magnets along path
- Use mirrors to extend sight lines
