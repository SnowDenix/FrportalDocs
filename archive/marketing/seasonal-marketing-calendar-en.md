## Purpose

This document provides the annual marketing calendar framework for all Aydınlı Group franchise operations. It ensures coordinated marketing activities across all brands and regions, maximizing commercial opportunities while maintaining brand consistency.

### Calendar Objectives

| Objective | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Alignment** | Synchronize marketing activities across all markets |
| **Planning** | Enable advance preparation for all campaigns |
| **Optimization** | Maximize commercial opportunities throughout the year |
| **Consistency** | Ensure unified brand messaging globally |
| **Efficiency** | Reduce last-minute preparations and costs |

## Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Season** | Major retail period (SS = Spring/Summer, FW = Fall/Winter) |
| **Campaign** | Coordinated marketing initiative with specific objectives |
| **Commercial Moment** | Key sales opportunity (holiday, event, sale period) |
| **Launch Window** | Period when new collection becomes available |
| **Sale Period** | Discounted pricing period for seasonal clearance |
| **Blackout Period** | Time when no promotions are allowed |
| **Peak Week** | Highest traffic/sales week within a period |
| **Lead Time** | Time required before campaign execution |

## Standards & Rules

### Calendar Compliance Rules

1. **Mandatory Campaigns:** All Tier 1 campaigns must be executed
2. **Timing:** Campaign start/end dates are fixed globally
3. **Messaging:** Use only approved campaign messaging
4. **Pricing:** Follow HQ-approved pricing and discounts
5. **Reporting:** Submit performance reports within 48 hours of campaign end

### Blackout Period Rules

| Blackout Type | Description | Duration |
|---------------|-------------|----------|
| **New Season Launch** | No discounts on new arrivals | First 4 weeks |
| **Before Major Sale** | No promotions before official sale | 2 weeks |
| **Brand Events** | Reserved for specific brand activities | As communicated |

### Promotional Calendar Rules

| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| Discount Limits | Maximum discount levels per period defined by HQ |
| Stacking | No combining multiple offers unless approved |
| Communication | All promotional messaging must be pre-approved |
| Duration | Promotional periods cannot be extended without approval |
| Coverage | Promotions apply to specified product categories only |

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Late Campaign Materials

**Symptom:** POSM and assets arrive after campaign start date.

**Causes:**
- Production delays
- Shipping issues
- Late approval

**Solution:**
1. Order materials 4+ weeks in advance
2. Use digital alternatives for day-one if physical delayed
3. Implement buffer time in planning
4. Have emergency local printing contacts

---

### Issue 2: Regional Calendar Conflicts

**Symptom:** Global campaign timing conflicts with regional events.

**Causes:**
- Islamic calendar variation
- Local holidays not considered
- Regional market differences

**Solution:**
1. Submit regional calendar conflicts early (Q4 previous year)
2. Request timing adaptation from HQ
3. Propose alternative execution dates
4. Document conflicts for future planning

---

### Issue 3: Inventory Misalignment

**Symptom:** Promoted products not available during campaign.

**Causes:**
- Forecasting errors
- Supply chain delays
- Poor communication with merchandising

**Solution:**
1. Share calendar with merchandising team monthly
2. Confirm stock levels 2 weeks before campaign
3. Identify backup products for promotion
4. Communicate stock issues immediately to marketing

---

### Issue 4: Campaign Fatigue

**Symptom:** Declining response to repeated promotional messages.

**Causes:**
- Too many campaigns
- Lack of variety in messaging
- Over-communication

**Solution:**
1. Space campaigns appropriately
2. Vary messaging and creative
3. Segment audience for targeted communications
4. Focus on quality over quantity
