## Purpose

This document establishes the standardized approval process for all marketing activities within the Aydınlı Grup franchise network. It ensures brand compliance, maintains quality standards, and provides clear guidelines for timely approval of marketing initiatives.

### Approval Process Objectives

| Objective | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Brand Protection** | Ensure all marketing aligns with brand standards |
| **Quality Assurance** | Maintain high-quality marketing outputs |
| **Efficiency** | Streamline approval workflows |
| **Accountability** | Clear ownership of decisions |
| **Compliance** | Meet legal and regulatory requirements |

## Scope

### Who This Document Covers

- **Store Managers:** Submitting local marketing requests
- **Regional Managers:** Reviewing and approving regional activities

## Definitions

| Term | Definition |
|------|------------|
| **Approval Request** | Formal submission seeking authorization for marketing activity |
| **Pre-Approval** | Authorization given before activity creation |
| **Final Approval** | Authorization for execution after review |
| **Conditional Approval** | Approval subject to specified modifications |
| **Rejection** | Denial of approval with documented reasons |
| **Escalation** | Moving request to higher authority level |
| **SLA** | Service Level Agreement - committed response time |
| **Brand Compliance** | Adherence to brand guidelines and standards |
| **Legal Review** | Assessment for legal and regulatory compliance |

### Creative Submission Standards

**Design Files:**
- Format: AI, PSD, PDF (editable)

- Color: CMYK for print, RGB for digital
- Fonts: Outlined or embedded
- Layers: Organized and labeled

- Clear and final text
- All language versions required
- Terms and conditions included
- Legal disclaimers where needed

### Review Criteria

| Criteria | Assessment Areas |
|----------|------------------|
| **Brand Alignment** | Logo usage, colors, messaging, tone |
| **Quality** | Design standards, production quality |
| **Compliance** | Legal requirements, regulatory standards |
| **Budget** | Cost reasonability, ROI potential |
| **Strategy** | Alignment with marketing objectives |
| **Timing** | Appropriateness of timing, calendar conflicts |

### Approval Statuses

| Status | Meaning | Next Steps |
|--------|---------|------------|
| **Submitted** | Request received | Awaiting initial review |
| **Under Review** | Being assessed | No action needed |
| **Additional Info Needed** | Missing information | Provide requested info |
| **Approved** | Full approval granted | Proceed with execution |
| **Conditionally Approved** | Approved with changes | Make specified changes |
| **Rejected** | Not approved | Review feedback, may resubmit |
| **On Hold** | Temporarily paused | Await further instruction |

## Turnaround Times

### Standard SLAs

| Approval Category | Initial Response | Final Decision |
|-------------------|------------------|----------------|
| **Category A (Standard)** | 24 hours | 2-3 business days |
| **Category B (Custom)** | 48 hours | 5-7 business days |
| **Category C (Strategic)** | 48 hours | 10-14 business days |
| **Category D (Legal)** | 48 hours | 7-14 business days |

### Expedited Process

**Eligibility:**
- Time-sensitive opportunity
- Competitive response needed
- External deadline driven

**Process:**
1. Mark request as "URGENT"
2. Include justification for expedite
3. Contact approving authority directly
4. Provide all materials complete

**Expedited SLAs:**
- Category A: Same day
- Category B: 2 business days
- Category C: 5 business days
- Category D: 5 business days (if possible)

## Process Steps

### Submitting a Request

```
Step 1: Determine Approval Category
        └── Review category definitions above
        ↓
Step 2: Gather Required Documentation
        └── Check requirements for your category
        ↓
Step 3: Complete Request Form
        └── Fill all required fields
        ↓
Step 4: Attach All Documents
        └── Design files, quotes, etc.
        ↓
Step 5: Submit via Approval System
        └── Marketing Portal or designated channel
        ↓
Step 6: Receive Confirmation
        └── Request ID and expected timeline
        ↓
Step 7: Track Status
        └── Monitor for updates or info requests
        ↓
Step 8: Respond to Queries
        └── Provide additional info if requested
        ↓
Step 9: Receive Decision
        └── Approval, conditions, or rejection
        ↓
Step 10: Execute (if approved)
        └── Implement as approved
```

### Handling Rejection

```
1. Review Rejection Feedback
   └── Understand specific reasons
       ↓
2. Assess Options
   ├── Can issues be addressed?
   ├── Is alternative approach possible?
   └── Should request be abandoned?
       ↓
3. If Resubmitting:
   ├── Address all feedback points
   ├── Reference original request
   └── Explain changes made
       ↓
4. If Not Resubmitting:
   └── Document decision and reasons
```

## Standards & Rules

### Approval Rules

| Rule | Requirement |
|------|-------------|
| **No Retroactive Approval** | All activities must be approved before execution |
| **Complete Submissions** | Incomplete requests will be returned |
| **Authority Limits** | Must be approved at appropriate level |
| **Documentation** | All approvals must be documented |
| **Validity Period** | Approvals valid for specified period only |
| **Material Changes** | Changes require re-approval |

### Compliance Requirements

**Brand Compliance:**
- All marketing must follow brand guidelines
- Logo and color accuracy mandatory
- Messaging must be approved
- Typography must be brand-approved

**Legal Compliance:**
- Promotional terms must be clear
- Disclaimers where required
- No misleading claims
- Competition law adherence

### Documentation Standards

**Request Documentation:**
- Complete request form
- All supporting materials
- Business justification
- Budget details

**Approval Documentation:**
- Approval decision
- Approving authority
- Date of approval
- Any conditions or notes

**Post-Activity Documentation:**
- Actual spend vs. budget
- Activity photos/evidence
- Performance results
- Learnings for future

## Common Issues & Solutions

### Issue 1: Delayed Approval

**Symptom:** Approval taking longer than SLA.

**Solutions:**
1. Check request is complete
2. Follow up with approving authority
3. Escalate if SLA significantly exceeded
4. Plan better lead time for future requests

---

### Issue 2: Incomplete Submissions

**Symptom:** Requests returned for additional information.

**Prevention:**
- Use submission checklist
- Review requirements before submitting
- Include all standard documents

**Solutions:**
1. Provide missing information promptly
2. Learn from feedback for future
3. Create department checklist

---

### Issue 3: Repeated Rejections

**Symptom:** Similar requests rejected multiple times.

**Solutions:**
1. Request meeting with approving authority
2. Understand rejection patterns
3. Adjust approach accordingly
4. Consider pre-submission consultation

---

### Issue 4: Emergency Requests

**Symptom:** Urgent need with no time for standard process.

**Solutions:**
1. Use expedited process
2. Contact decision-maker directly
3. Provide complete justification
4. Accept potential limitations
