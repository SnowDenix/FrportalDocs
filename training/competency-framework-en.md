## Purpose

This document is designed to track the competency development of personnel working in Aydınlı Group franchise stores, plan career paths, and establish a standard progression system.

**Core Objectives:**
- Define clear competency levels
- Make personnel development measurable
- Provide roadmap for career advancement
- Systematically identify training needs

## Learning Objectives

Personnel who understand this competency framework will:

### Knowledge Level
- [ ] Know the 5 competency levels and their characteristics
- [ ] Can explain requirements of each level
- [ ] Define progression criteria
- [ ] Understand assessment process

### Skill Level
- [ ] Can accurately assess their own level
- [ ] Can identify development areas
- [ ] Can create progression plan

### Behavior Level
- [ ] Maintains continuous development motivation
- [ ] Works toward next level
- [ ] Contributes to others' development

## Common Mistakes and Coaching Notes

### Common Mistakes in Competency Development

| Mistake | Result | Correction |
|---------|--------|------------|
| Trying to skip levels | Failure | Step-by-step progression |
| Not getting feedback | Development slows | Request regular feedback |
| Focusing only on sales | One-dimensional | Develop all competencies |
| Refusing mentorship | Missing opportunity | Be open to learning |

### Coaching Tips

```
FOR LEVEL 1-2:
• Focus on basic skills
• Be patient, turn mistakes into learning
• Celebrate small wins
• Give regular feedback

FOR LEVEL 3-4:
• Set advanced goals
• Create mentorship opportunities
• Develop leadership skills
• Give challenging tasks

FOR LEVEL 5:
• Encourage strategic thinking
• Offer training opportunities
• Do career planning
• Assign regional level projects
```
